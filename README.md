# ToadsWildAdventure
Toads Wild Adventure is a family-friendly puzzle and arcade game where players guide Frog safely home through a whimsical pond world.

## Overview

Every move matters. Players hop across a pond board, avoid hazards, use helpful creatures and special moves, earn stars, build perfect streaks, and unlock achievement illustrations.

The game also includes Fly Feast, a fast mini-game where Frog catches flies with a stretchy tongue and can earn an Owl Immunity Pass for the main game.

## How to Play

### Main Goal

Guide Frog safely back to the pond.

Plan each move carefully. The safest path is not always the shortest path.

### Watch Out

- Owls can catch Frog.
- Storks fly across the board after a warning.
- Rocks block movement.
- Water may require help from turtles, diving, or special movement.
- Poor route choices can cost stars or end a perfect streak.

### Helpful Friends and Items

- Turtles help Frog travel across water.
- Fireflies can help with special movement.
- Magical flies can unlock powerful movement or special effects.
- Owl Immunity Pass protects Frog from one owl catch.

### Stars and Streaks

Players earn stars by completing levels efficiently.

- 3 stars: best route / perfect result
- 2 stars: completed, but not the best route
- 1 star: completed with help or extra moves

Perfect 3-star levels build a streak. Streaks can unlock special achievement illustrations.

### Fly Feast

Fly Feast is a separate mini-game where Frog catches flies.

- Drag left or right to move Frog.
- Tap to flick Frog's tongue.
- Magical flies can add extra frogs.
- Catch enough flies to earn FrogFeastMaster and receive an Owl Immunity Pass.

## Difficulty Modes

### Normal

A balanced pond puzzle experience for casual play.

### Grand Master

A harder version of the game with tougher puzzles and more pressure, while still designed to remain fair and solvable.

## Features
